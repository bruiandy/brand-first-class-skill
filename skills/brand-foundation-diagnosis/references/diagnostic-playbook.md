# Diagnostic Playbook

## Inputs This Skill Can Handle

- A messy founder story
- A structured questionnaire
- Discovery-call notes
- A real brand case written in free text
- A short description of "what stopped working"

## First-Pass Question Set

When the user gives too little context, start with the fewest high-leverage questions possible. You usually do not need all of these.

1. 你现在做的是什么品类，品牌大概处在什么阶段？
2. 你现在最主要的渠道是什么，过去什么打法开始失效了？
3. 你们最强的资源是什么，最弱的约束是什么？
4. 现在卖得最好的产品或 SKU 是什么，用户为什么会买？
5. 你现在最想解决的是增长、转化、复购、品牌表达，还是团队方向？
6. 你过去 3-6 个月已经做过哪些尝试，哪些有效，哪些无效？

## How To Triage The Problem

### More Likely a Resource Problem

- The team wants to do more than the current long board can support.
- The user cannot name a real advantage that affects acquisition or distribution.
- The best actions are still disconnected from any unique asset.

### More Likely an Operating Problem

- Margin, channels, or category structure make the desired brand move unrealistic.
- The founder wants "brand growth" without facing pricing, channel, or supply constraints.
- The real issue is business design, not expression.

### More Likely a Brand/Product Problem

- The team cannot explain the core value or target scene clearly.
- The product is being sold by feature list, not by job or transition.
- Narrative and experience do not support each other.

### More Likely a Closed-Loop Problem

- The team is spread across too many channels.
- Exposure exists but repeatable conversion logic is weak.
- Content volume is growing but loop efficiency is unclear.

## Collaboration Fit Signals

### Higher Fit

- The user can name real facts, not only slogans.
- The user accepts tradeoffs and is willing to focus.
- There is at least one usable long board.
- The founder is open to adjusting sequence, not only tactics.

### Lower Fit

- The user wants a quick traffic hack but refuses to discuss operating reality.
- The team wants everything at once.
- The founder treats brand as a magic solution for every business issue.
- There is no willingness to prioritize or pause.

## Output Template

Use a structure close to this:

- `现状判断`：用 2-4 句话总结阶段和当前局面
- `最强资源 / 最大约束`：各写 1-2 条
- `核心问题`：列 1-3 条最关键判断
- `问题归因`：更像认知、资源、经营、品牌、产品、渠道、内容还是组织
- `先做什么`：给出 2-4 条优先动作
- `先别做什么`：明确 1-3 条暂时不要做的事
- `下一轮要补的信息`：列出最值得继续问的 3 个点

## Collaboration Support Outputs

After the first diagnosis, this skill can also produce:

- a short memo for the next meeting
- a sharper problem statement
- a 30/60/90 day direction
- follow-up questions tailored to the user's stage

Do not turn every answer into a huge report. Keep the next step clear enough that the user can act on it.
